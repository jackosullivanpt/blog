The Elixir Foundation

The aim is to give children and young adults a better chance of leading healthier happier lives and to give them a greater chance of success in life by creating an environment whereby they can achieve their dreams.


We aim to get more children and young adults moving by getting them outside running around and playing like children are supposed to do.


Objectives

To provide fitness programs in schools and colleges. Our goal isn’t just to fight against obesity, it is to improve a child's physical and mental health, and improve their test results so that they get an overall better chance to succeed in life.


To encourage children to get outdoors and play outside more often while educating them about the environment and how their actions now can benefit the planet in the future and create a better world for their children.


To provide safer parks and open spaces for children to play in.


Youth Athlete Development and Advance Progressive Training program

With an abundance of sporting talent in the UK, it would be a dream of mine to set up the You ADAPT program.


To get the project underway we are looking to raise £10,000 which will be spent on equipment and coaches. This will allow us to run community projects in schools and summer camps.
