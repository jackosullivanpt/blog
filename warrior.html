I cater for all levels from beginners to elite athletes.

There is a minimum standard of fitness for each level that will allow you to be better prepared for the run:


The Circuit

There are 5 exerices - Squats, Crunches, Press ups, Burpees, Star jumps.

The whole circuit is to be completed within 5 minutes and each exericse must be completed with 1 minute


  • Level 1 - Beginner

    Run: 5km in 40 minutes

    Circuit: 30 / 20 / 10 / 5 / 10


  • Level 2 - Intermidiate

    Run: 8km in 45 minutes

    Circuit: 50 / 40 / 20 / 10 / 20


  • Level 3 - Advance

    Run: 10km in 50 minutes

    Circuit: 80 / 60 / 30 / 15 / 30


  • Level 4 - Expert

    Run: 12.5km in 55 minutes

    Circuit: 100 / 80 / 40 / 20 / 40


  • Level 5 - Elite

    Run: 15km in 60 minutes

    Circuit: 120 / 100 / 50 / 25 / 50



There is no need to worry if you don't think you are at a certain level yet. When you book a Primal Warrior Run I provide a training program to get your fitness up to the next level you are looking to conquer.
