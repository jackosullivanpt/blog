The Move Around Project

The aim is to give children and young adults a better chance of leading healthier happier lives and to give them a greater chance of success in life by creating an environment whereby they can achieve their dreams.


We aim to get more children and young adults moving by getting them outside running around and playing like humans are supposed to.

To provide safer parks and open spaces for children to play in.


Youth Athlete Development Program

With an abundance of sporting talent in the UK, it would be a dream of mine to set up a Youth Athlete Development Program through the move around project.


This will allow young people to get the correct training and guidance to become the best athletes that they can be.
