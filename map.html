The Move Around Project

The aim is to give children and young adults a better chance of leading healthier happier lives and to give them a greater chance of success in life


We aim to get more children and young adults moving by getting them outside running around and playing like humans are supposed to


Get children and young adults involved in small sided games and make it fun for everyone involved, especially the less active children
